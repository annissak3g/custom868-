Section 01

Introduction

Playbeat Digital ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, use our platforms, or engage with our services.

By using any Playbeat Digital service, you consent to the data practices described in this Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of our services.

Our Commitment: We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their own marketing purposes. Your data is used solely to deliver and improve our services.
Section 02

Information We Collect

We collect information in several ways depending on how you interact with our services:

Category Examples How Collected
Identity Data Name, username, profile information Account registration
Contact Data Email address, phone number, WhatsApp Registration, contact forms
Payment Data Transaction records, billing details Purchase flow (via payment processors)
Technical Data IP address, browser type, device info, OS Automatically via logs & analytics
Usage Data Pages visited, features used, session duration Automatically via cookies & tracking
Communications Data Support messages, feedback, correspondence Direct contact with our team
We do not collect sensitive data such as racial or ethnic origin, political opinions, religious beliefs, health data, or biometric data as part of our standard services.
Section 03

How We Use Your Data

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

Service Delivery — To create and manage your account, process transactions, and provide ordered services
Communication — To respond to enquiries, send service-related notifications, and provide support
Security & Fraud Prevention — To detect, investigate, and prevent unauthorized access, fraud, and abuse
Service Improvement — To analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, and improve user experience across our platforms
Legal Compliance — To comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal processes, or enforceable government requests
Marketing — To send promotional materials about our services where you have opted in or where permitted by law
Section 04

Legal Basis for Processing

Where applicable data protection laws require a lawful basis for processing personal data, we rely on the following:

Contract Performance
Processing necessary to fulfill a contract with you
Legitimate Interests
Fraud prevention, security, service improvement
Legal Obligation
Compliance with applicable laws and regulations
Consent
Where you have given explicit consent (e.g. marketing)
Section 05

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

Service Providers — Trusted third-party vendors (payment processors, cloud hosts, analytics) who process data strictly on our behalf and under confidentiality obligations
Legal Requirements — When required by law, court order, or government authority
Business Transfers — In connection with a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, subject to the same privacy commitments
With Your Consent — In any other circumstances where you have given explicit permission
Section 06

Cookies & Tracking

Our website and services use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience and analyze site usage.

Cookie Type Purpose Can Be Disabled?
Essential Required for core functionality (authentication, security, sessions) No — required for service
Analytics Understand how users interact with our platforms (e.g. Google Analytics) Yes — via browser settings
Functional Remember preferences such as language, region, or display settings Yes — via browser settings
Marketing Deliver relevant advertisements and track campaign effectiveness Yes — opt-out available
You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling non-essential cookies will not affect core service functionality.
Section 07

Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including legal, accounting, or reporting requirements.

Account data is retained for the duration of your account and for a reasonable period after deletion to satisfy legal obligations
Transaction and billing records are retained for up to 7 years to comply with financial regulations
Support communications are retained for a reasonable period to improve service quality and resolve disputes
Usage and analytics data may be retained in anonymized or aggregated form indefinitely
Section 08

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Encryption
Data encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest
Access Controls
Role-based access limiting data exposure to authorized personnel
Secure Infrastructure
Hosted on reputable cloud platforms with industry-standard security certifications
Regular Audits
Periodic security reviews and vulnerability assessments
Important: No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security. Please use strong passwords and protect your account credentials.

Section 10

Third-Party Links

Our website and services may contain links to third-party websites, integrations, or platforms. These external sites have their own privacy policies and we are not responsible for their content or data practices.

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you interact with through our platforms, including payment processors and communication tools.

Section 11

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

Notice: If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data without parental consent, please contact us immediately at support@playbeat.digital and we will take steps to delete that information promptly.
Section 12

International Transfers

Your personal data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have different data protection laws than your own.

When we transfer data internationally, we take appropriate safeguards to ensure your information is protected in accordance with this Privacy Policy, including the use of standard contractual clauses or reliance on adequacy decisions where applicable.

We encourage you to review this Policy periodically. Continued use of our services after any updates constitutes acceptance of the revised Policy.
